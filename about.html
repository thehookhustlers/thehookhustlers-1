Our Mission

Democratising Access to Capital for Every Indian Startup

India has over 6 lakh registered startups. Less than 2% ever get funded. The system was broken — opaque, metro-biased and inaccessible to founders from Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities who have incredible ideas but no connections.

The Hook Hustlers exists to change that. We built a platform where any DPIIT-verified startup — from Lucknow to Vishakhapatnam, from Indore to Imphal — can connect directly with the right investor, at the right time, for the right amount.

No gatekeepers. No geography bias. Just great startups meeting great capital.

"I was 15 when I tried to get funding for my first idea. The system was broken, the doors were closed, and nobody in Lucknow knew where to even start. So I stopped knocking — and built my own door."

Class 10. One Idea. Zero Guidance.

Aniket Singh was in Class 10 when he had his first real business idea. Not a school project — an actual concept he believed could work. He started researching how to raise money, how to find investors, how to structure a startup. What he found was a wall. Every resource assumed you were already in Bangalore or Mumbai. Every investor platform was built for people who already had connections. Every guide assumed you had a CA, a lawyer, and a network.

He had none of that. He had an idea, a laptop, and a lot of stubbornness.

The Problem Became the Product

Two years of frustration became the clearest product brief in the world. By the time he was 17 and had just completed Class 12, Aniket had a very precise understanding of exactly what was missing — not just for himself, but for hundreds of thousands of founders across India who were stuck in the same position.

The gap wasn't effort. It wasn't talent. It was access — structured, verified, geography-agnostic access between startups and investors. That's the only thing The Hook Hustlers is built to provide.
