The Hook Hustlers was born from frustration — and transformed into India's most ambitious startup-investor matchmaking platform.

Our Mission

Democratising Access to Capital for Every Indian Startup

India has over 6 lakh registered startups. Less than 2% ever get funded. The system was broken — opaque, metro-biased and inaccessible to founders from Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities who have incredible ideas but no connections.

The Hook Hustlers exists to change that. We built a platform where any DPIIT-verified startup — from Varanasi to Vishakhapatnam, from Indore to Imphal — can connect directly with the right investor, at the right time, for the right amount.

No gatekeepers. No geography bias. Just great startups meeting great capital.

The Founder

Aniket Singh

Chief Hustler & Founder · Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh 🇮🇳

"I was 17 when I tried to get funding for my idea. The system was broken, the doors were closed. So I stopped knocking — and built my own door. Hook Hustlers is for every Indian startup that deserves better."

Growing up in Varanasi, Aniket Singh always had big dreams — but when he tried to get funding for his first idea fresh out of Class 12, he hit wall after wall. The Indian funding ecosystem was opaque, metro-biased and completely inaccessible to a young founder from UP with no connections.

Instead of giving up, he turned his frustration into fuel. At 17, he started building The Hook Hustlers — a platform designed to be everything the Indian funding ecosystem wasn't: transparent, accessible, Pan India, and genuinely founder-friendly. He believes that India's next great companies are being born in Jaipur, Lucknow, Bhopal and Nagpur — not just Bangalore and Mumbai — and they all deserve a fair shot at capital.
