"I was 17 when I tried to get funding for my idea. The system was broken, the doors were closed. So I stopped knocking — and built my own door. Hook Hustlers is for every Indian startup that deserves better."

At just 17, fresh out of Class 12, Aniket Singh experienced firsthand the frustration that thousands of Indian founders face every day — a broken, opaque, metro-biased funding ecosystem that shuts out brilliant ideas from Bharat's heartland. Instead of accepting the status quo, he decided to build India's most ambitious startup-investor matchmaking platform from scratch. The Hook Hustlers is his answer to a system that wasn't built for founders like him — and like you.
