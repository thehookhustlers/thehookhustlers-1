The Problem We Solve

India's Funding Gap is Real

Hundreds of thousands of brilliant startups across India never find the right investor — not because their idea is bad, but because the system is broken. We're fixing that.

🔍

No Discovery

98% of Indian startups have no structured way to get in front of the right investor. Cold emails go unanswered. Warm intros require connections most founders don't have.

🏙️

Metro Bias

48% of India's startups are from Tier 2 & 3 cities — but nearly all funding goes to Bangalore, Mumbai and Delhi. Geography shouldn't determine destiny.

🔒

Opaque Process

The funding process in India is largely relationship-driven and opaque. First-time founders have no visibility into who is investing, at what stage, and what they're looking for.

⚡

The Hook Hustlers Fix

We built a verified, AI-powered, Pan India platform that gives every DPIIT-registered startup — regardless of city or connections — a direct path to the right investor.
